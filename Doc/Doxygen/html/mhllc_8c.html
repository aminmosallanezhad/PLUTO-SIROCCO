Detailed Description

Solve the Riemann problem for the resistive relativistic MHD (ResRMHD) equations of Mignone et al. (2019).

Reference:

  • "A constrained transport method for the solution of the resistive relativistic MHD equations", Mignone et al., MNRAS (2019) 486, 4252.
