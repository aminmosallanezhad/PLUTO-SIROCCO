Detailed Description

Compute interface states using the 5th-order monotonicity-preserving schemes of Suresh & Huynh (1997) or WENO-Z (Borges et al. 2008).

Author
A. Mignone (andre.nosp@m.a.mi.nosp@m.gnone.nosp@m.@uni.nosp@m.to.it)
Date
Sep 06, 2024

References

  • "An improved weighted essentially non-oscillatory scheme for hyperbolic conservation laws" Borges et al, JCP (2008) 227, 3191
