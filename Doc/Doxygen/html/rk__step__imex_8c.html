Detailed Description

Main driver for SSP IMEX Runge-Kutta time evolution. Time stepping include:

RK2: IMEX-SSP2 (2,2,2) RK3: IMEX-SSP3 (3,3,2) RK4: IMEX-SSP3 (4,3,3)

  • "Implicit-Explicit Runge-Kutta Schemes and Applications to Hyperbolic Systems with Relaxation", Journal Sci. Comput (2005)
  • "Beyond ideal MHD: towards a more realistic modelling of relativistic astrophysical plasmas"
    Palenzuela & al, RAS (2009) 394, 1727-1740
