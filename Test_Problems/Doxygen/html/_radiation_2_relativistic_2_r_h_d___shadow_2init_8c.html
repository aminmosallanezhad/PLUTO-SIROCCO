Sets up a 2D problem in which a free-streaming radiation field encounters a highly opaque region of space, casting a shadow behind it.

Only the upper half of the elliptic region is considered in Configurations #1 and #3, where reflective boundary conditions have been imposed at the bottom boundary. Conversely, the entire elliptic region is considered in Configuration #2, where outflow conditions are imposed in all boundaries but the left one.
